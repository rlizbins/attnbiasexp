Attention Bias Experiment (WebGazer)

Purpose

  • To determine if the participant demonstrates any level of attention bias on certain types of images.
  • The experiment will be conducted using the WebGazer.js eye tracking library

Procedures

  1. Once you are ready to begin the experiment, click on the green below.
  2. At the center of the screen you will see two images. Try to focus and analyze the features of one image.
  3. You should not have to press or click any buttons for the duration of the experiment.
  4. The images will change every 2 seconds, so make sure to stay focused.
  5. Click the appropriate buttons at the bottom if needed to pause or return to the main menu.
  6. Thank you for your time.

Rules

  • Just sit back and relax, but make sure to stay focused on the images shown on the screen
